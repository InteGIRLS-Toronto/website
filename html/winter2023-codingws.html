Welcome to the Intro to Coding Workshop, presented by the Dandelion Project and INTEGIRLS Toronto!

The Dandelion Project Ontario and INTEGIRLS Toronto are excited to announce that we will be hosting a virtual introductory coding workshop on February 26th at 1 PM. This 90 minute workshop is geared towards high school students interested in learning more about the field and gaining some beginner Python experience. Two Queen's University graduate computer science students will lead this interactive workshop, with the ultimate goal of students coding a small project during the workshop. We hope to see you there!

Make sure to follow us on Instagram @dandelionprojectontario & @integirls.toronto and join our Discord: bit.ly/integirls_discord!
