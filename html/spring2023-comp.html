Welcome to the INTEGIRLS Toronto Spring 2023 Math Competition!

The Spring 2023 competition will be held IN-PERSON at Trafalgar Castle School (401 Reynolds St. Whitby, ON L1N 3W9). Doors will open at 11:00 am for registration and the competition will start at 12:00 pm. Some snacks will be provided, prizes can be won, and there will be giveaways as well!!

The cost to participate in the competition is completely free. You just need to bring some snacks, water, a pencil and eraser, and group of friends!

This is a team based competition, with up to 4 members per team. If you are registering yourself, we will add you to a team. Accordingly, if you do not have 4 members in your team, we will add members. Each group member must be in the same division (canâ€™t have high school and elementary school students in one group).

Only ONE member per team needs to fill this form out. Each ticket admission = 1 member (eg. if you are registering for a team of 4, you will be purchasing 4 ticket admissions).

NOTE:
  • All teams must have at least 2 female or non-binary members. Additionally, all participants must be in elementary school (grade 1-8) or high school (grade 9-12).
  • It is recommended that elementary students are in grade 5-8 to participate in the competition.
