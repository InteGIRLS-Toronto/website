Welcome to the INTEGIRLS Toronto Spring 2024 Math Competition! Put your skills to the test and win cash prizes (over $200 total prize money!)

The Spring 2024 competition will be held on May 26th, 2024 online, from 12PM-4PM. There is NO cost - all you need to bring is an enthusiasm for problem-solving! Join us for an exciting day of problem solving, guest speakers, and games!

This is a team-based competition, and participants are welcome to register either individually or in teams of up to 4. We offer both a middle school (grades 5-8) and high school (grades 9-12) division, and all team members must be in the same division. Individual competitors can opt to be randomly assigned to a team if they wish.

NOTE:
  • Participants must not participate in the 2024 spring math competition for any other INTEGIRLS chapter.
  • All participants must be Canadian and must be girl or non-binary individuals comfortable being grouped with girls in elementary school (grades 1-8) or high school (grades 9-12)
  • It is recommended that elementary students wishing to participate be in grades 5-8.

Only ONE member per team needs to fill out the form below.
