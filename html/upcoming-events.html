Upcoming Events

Welcome to the INTEGIRLS Toronto Fall 2023 Math Competition!

The Fall 2023 competition will be held VIRTUALLY through Zoom on November 25th starting 12:30 PM EST, and end at around 5:00 PM. There is NO cost. You just need a computer and wi-fi!

We have over $1250 worth of prizes thanks to our sponsors: the Daily Challenge with Po-Shen Loh, Wolfram Alpha, and the Actuarial Foundation of Canada.

This is a team-based competition with up to 4 members per team. There will be two divisions: an elementary school (grades 1-8) and high school (grades 9-12) division. All group members must be in the same division. Individual competitors are welcome, but you can opt to be randomly assigned to a team if you don't have one!

NOTE:
  • All participants must be Canadian and must be girl or non-binary individuals comfortable being grouped with girls in elementary school (grades 1-8) or high school (grades 9-12)
  • It is recommended that elementary students wishing to participate be in grades 5-8.

Only ONE member per team needs to fill out the form below.
