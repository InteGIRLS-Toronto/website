Description

Welcome to the InteGIRLS Toronto Fall 2022 Math Competition!

The Fall 2022 competition will be held VIRTUALLY through Zoom on November 26th starting from 12:30 PM EST. The cost to participate in the competition is completely free. You just need a computer, wi-fi, and a group of friends!

This is a team based competition, with up to 4 members per team. Competing by yourself is allowed but is not recommended. Each group member must be in the same division (can't have high school and elementary school students in one group).

Only ONE member per team needs to fill this form out.

NOTE: All participants must be living in Canada and must be a female or non-binary student in elementary school (grade 1-8) or high school (grade 9-12). However, it is highly recommended that elementary students are in grade 5-8 to participate in the competition.

Make sure to follow us on Instagram @integirls.toronto and join our Discord bit.ly/integirls_discord

Contact us at integirlstoronto.logistics@gmail.com for any questions or concerns.
